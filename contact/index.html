Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Edgewood Park

How do you typically structure engagements?

Engagements are customized based on your specific needs. Product marketing projects typically run 3-6 months, while fractional CMO arrangements are ongoing with 6-month minimums. We'll discuss the best structure during our initial consultation.

What's your experience with B2B software companies?

I've worked with 50+ B2B software companies across various stages, from early-stage startups to enterprise platforms. My experience spans SaaS, enterprise software, developer tools, and AI/ML platforms.

How quickly can we get started?

After our initial consultation, most projects can begin within 1-2 weeks. The timeline depends on project scope and current availability. For urgent needs, expedited starts may be possible.

Do you work with specific company sizes?

I work with companies from Series A startups to public enterprises. The key is having a product-market fit foundation and readiness to scale go-to-market efforts strategically.
